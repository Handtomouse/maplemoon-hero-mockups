01 — Origin

It starts with the pod

Carob grows wild across the Mediterranean — a sun-loving tree that thrives without irrigation. Each pod is hand-harvested, dried slowly, and selected for sweetness. No caffeine. No bitterness. Just warmth.

02 — Craft

Roasted by hand in Byron Bay

We roast in small batches to draw out deep caramel and malt notes. The carob is stone-ground, then blended with organic cacao butter — nothing else. Every bar is shaped, wrapped, and packed by hand.

03 — The Bar

Simple ingredients, nothing hidden

Organic carob. Organic cacao butter. That's it. No emulsifiers, no preservatives, no refined sugar. Each bar snaps clean with a velvet melt — proof that less makes more.

04 — Shop

Find your flavour

Six bars. Two elixirs. One mission — to make something good enough that you forget it's good for you.
