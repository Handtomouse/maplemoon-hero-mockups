## Carob Bars — $12.95, 80g

### Original Pure Carob

**Short:** Australian carob and cacao butter, hand-tempered and set. Nothing else.

**Medium:** Our original bar is pure carob and cacao butter. No sugar, no sweetener, no flavouring. Hand-tempered in Byron Bay using Australian-grown carob that's naturally sweet on its own. This is where it all starts.

**Long:** The Original Pure Carob bar is MapleMoon in its simplest form. Australian-grown carob, slow-roasted and milled, combined with organic cacao butter and hand-tempered in our Byron Bay kitchen. There's nothing added because nothing needs to be. Carob is naturally sweet, rich in flavour, and entirely free from caffeine. Each 80g bar is wrapped by hand and made in small batches. If you've never tried carob before, start here.

### Spiced Pepperberry

**Short:** Warm native pepperberry through pure carob. A quiet heat that lingers.

**Medium:** Our Spiced Pepperberry bar pairs Australian carob with native pepperberry for a gentle warmth that builds slowly. Organic, vegan, and caffeine-free. The spice is subtle — it doesn't shout, it stays.

**Long:** Native Australian pepperberry meets pure carob and cacao butter in a bar that warms from the inside. The pepperberry is ground fine and folded through each batch, so the heat is even and gentle — a slow bloom rather than a bite. Handmade in Byron Bay, organic, and completely caffeine-free. For those who like their carob with a little edge.

### Coffee
